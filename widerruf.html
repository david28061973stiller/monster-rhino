Custom Preparation

Each Monster Rhino kitbase is individually prepared, sourced, printed, partially assembled and checked for the respective customer. No inventory is kept. Production starts only after direct communication and order confirmation.

No Automated Sale

There is no shopping cart and no automated checkout. Every request is discussed individually. Scope of service, limitations, third-party components and safety aspects are clarified before production starts.

Cancellation and Returns

Where the legal requirements for goods made to customer specification or clearly tailored to personal needs are met, a statutory right of cancellation may be excluded. Returns, cancellations or refunds are therefore generally not intended once production has started. Statutory rights in case of defects remain unaffected.

Damage or Manufacturing Defect

If a kitbase arrives damaged or shows a manufacturing defect, please contact me directly. The aim is always to find a fair and practical solution.
